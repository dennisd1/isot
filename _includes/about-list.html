        First time CSA-er over here.

        As a newbie, I had no idea if a CSA would be a good fit for my lifestyle, so I wanted to try it out before committing. As it turns out, most places require you to sign up for a weekly or bi-weekly delivery, but Greenhearts lets you do a one-time delivery for $38.

        I cook 3-5x a week and keep things pretty simple. I tend to repeat the same go-to meals, but I wanted to mix things up and incorporate new fruits and veggies into my diet. With all the five-star reviews, Greenhearts seemed like a good place to start!

        A few days before the delivery, I received an email listing out the items I would be receiving. This helped me envision some meals I could make.

        The box arrived on Thursday and I unpacked the following:
        - Fennel
        - Jonagold apples
        - Sharlyn melon
        - Oyster mushrooms
        - Spinach
        - Red leaf lettuce
        - Gold nugget squash
        - Gold beets
        - Nantes carrots
        - Red bell pepper
        - Heirloom tomatoes

        Things I made: roasted chicken with fennel, roasted squash & beets, heirloom toast, egg scramble with oyster mushrooms & spinach, pesto pasta with bell pepper & chicken sausage, and more. The fruits were great to snack on.

        Honestly, everything was amazing! The gold beets and carrots were super sweet. The squash was the best squash I've ever had. I polished off the apples within two days. It was a bit intimidating at first but ultimately I'm so glad I went with Greenhearts for my first CSA and I can't wait to try it again. I would love to order the eggs (they cost extra) too.

        Just the other night my fiancé asked, "When are we getting the next CSA?"

        I don't often leave Yelp reviews, but I've been so impressed by my experience with Greenhearts I just couldn't let it go unheralded.         I LOVE GREENHEARTS.

        I found Greenhearts Family Farm through Yelp, so thought I should return the…         I LOVE GREENHEARTS.

        I found Greenhearts Family Farm through Yelp, so thought I should return the favor.

        We were looking for: high quality, in a quantity we could manage, local, and good for the earth. I am 100% satisfied with Greenhearts' delivery in all of these items. Delivery to our door is a super bonus!

        We have received 4 boxes, and just placed another order. We are a busy family of 2 adults who are trying to incorporate more healthy food into our lives. We get 1 box delivered every other week. We get the list of what's in the box the day before delivery, and do some planning to eat as much as possible. We do a pretty good job of eating the whole box, but a weekly delivery would be too much for us. 1 of us is a vegetarian.

        I am consistently impressed by:

        1) The quality of the produce. We have bought the same items on the same day from a supermarket (wanted to make extra), and the supermarket items go bad while the Greenhearts produce will last a week or two longer (depending on the item)!

        2) The amount of food for the price. You cannot beat a box brimming with fresh, delicious, food for $35. Two weeks worth of food! We would purchase the traditional CSA share to support the farm, but can't afford it at this time. You can't beat the price and convenience of this week by week delivery option.

        3) Delivery right to our door for no extra cost? With these fuel prices? Thank you!

        Customer Service: We live in an impossibly hard to find location. The first week the box did not arrive to our door, and we never heard who it went to. I called Aurora the next day. She did her due diligence to see if they were able to make the delivery, and was very apologetic when she called back. I supplied pictures of how to get to our impossibly hidden home, and another box arrived to our door the following week at no extra charge. Aurora was very kind and understanding about the snafu. I have heard horror stories about similar situations with Farm Fresh to You. Just go with Greenhearts!

        Ordering the first box took a bit of effort. They have two websites, and I think the request got pushed back a bit. I got the best results by ordering through csasanfrancisco.com. Once they picked up the request, they were very responsive to my many emails asking many more questions and everything was good from there.

        We are very lucky to have Paul and Aurora so close. We hope to be customers for many more years!         Read more

        Though I've been basically vegetarian for decades now, I don't much like shopping or cooking. I love Rainbow Grocery and I frequent Whole Foods, but there's always too much to choose from and often what I buy may not be the healthiest choices for myself or the planet, or I spend too much and feel guilty. But I live alone and someone's gotta do it. And when I came down sick with a bad cold recently it was a wake-up call that I need to eat more fresh fruits and veggies (and spend less on eating out). So I decided organic produce home delivery would be a good thing. I used to work for one of these delivery services back in the 90s (they've since closed down), and I think it's a great business model. I checked Yelp and chose Greenhearts for all the five-star reviews. I made the right choice.

        I've had two deliveries so far and I totally love what I'm getting. Fresh, local, in-season, organically grown and tasty, what's not to like? Now I have so many appealing veggies in my fridge, I'm eating lots of them daily out of a sense of duty (not wanting stuff to spoil) and loving it besides. I'm spending way less on eating out, and I'm enjoying the sense of pride that comes with supporting local organic farms and sustainable business enterprise.

        With the first box I made the mistake of eating my favorites first and unfortunately a couple of the items started to spoil before I got to cooking them. This time I'm reading the mini-newsletter that comes with each box and paying attention to which items are most perishable so that I consume those first. I think the every-other-week delivery option is going to work out very well for my single-person household, and I'm planning on taking out a subscription in mid-January when the regular delivery schedule resumes after the holiday interruptions.

        The only criticism I would make is that they apparently are not so responsive to emails and voicemails. I get the impression it's such a small staff that they don't have time. So if I have a need for communication, I call on a weekday when Aurora is there to answer the phone and she handles anything on the spot. Very good people, and I'm so grateful they're doing what they're doing for the benefit of us city folk and the Earth we all share.

      Specialties

      Greenhearts Family Farm has emerged as the Bay Area's most loved and highly recommended CSA home delivery service for fresh organics. Why? Because we are organic farmers first and foremost with a lifelong connection to the best local organic farmers.

      We take an intensely personal approach to feeding you and your family only what we grow and eat ourselves. And our farm is a place you'll take great pride in visiting and calling your own!

      We offer the ultimate in farm fresh, organic, pasture raised meats and eggs and artisan produce delivered right to your door.

      Greenhearts is not looking for customers! We are seeking community partners - folks who want to be part of our farm's CSA, through the successes and failures, in good times and bad.

      So join us at the farmer's table. Eat like you're an organic farmer- fresh, ripe, beautiful, pasture raised, real!

      Greenhearts Family Farm CSA - Your Local Farmer's Market Delivered!

      History

      Established in 2007.

      Farmers Paul and Aurora fell in love while working on organic farms across the globe. They soon started their own business growing and selling organic produce and pasture raised eggs and chickens at farmer's markets across the Bay Area and delivering their beautiful veggie boxes to a handful of neighbors.

      The popularity of Greenhearts Family Farm quickly grew, and it wasn't long before the two young farmers expanded their farming venture, hiring an amazing crew to help grow more food and deliver across the Bay Area.

      Having studied and lived with organic pioneers and farm families across the world, inheriting the honored traditions and methods of natural farming, Greenhearts Family Farm is a work of art and an act of love, never simply a business.

      Paul & Aurora have dedicated their lives to community supported organic agriculture. Get to know them personally when you visit their spectacular Half Moon Bay farm and begin receiving their beloved CSA box!
